Retail Demand Forecasting and Inventory Replenishment Optimization

This project analyzes retail sales and inventory data to identify demand patterns, detect stockouts, forecast product demand, and recommend optimal replenishment quantities.

Key Outcomes:
- Identification of high-risk SKUs causing stockouts
- Forecasting of demand trends using historical sales
- Replenishment model to determine reorder points and recommended order quantities
- Business insights to improve inventory availability and reduce lost sales
